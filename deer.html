This litle guy dressed out about 150 lbs. That's not bad for southern New York State.
He's a swamp buck given the dark rack. Probably about three years old. Not the biggest deer,
on the mountain but a nearly perfect rack. We named him "Stupid" since the first shot through brush
deflected, whereupon he ran right towards me. I had to yell at him to get him to stop and turn to get
a broadside shot at him.
