Michael's Class Project: A Box Harp

Michael wanted to make a guitar for his 3rd grade music instrument project. Knowing that was a little ambitious we talked him into a simple Box Harp. Except for the cutting on the table saw he did all the work himself. I provided a little tool guidance (tool protection) and the occasional third hand. We were both rather impressed with how the final result looked and sounded.
