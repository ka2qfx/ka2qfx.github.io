IRISH POTATOE BREAD

Too easy for this collection. But it’s good.

 

 

3 cups Bisquick®

1 5oz. box of instant mashed potatoes

1 cup raisins

2 tbsp sugar

2 tsp caraway seed

2 cups milk

 

     Mix all ingredients well.  Mound into 9" greased cake

pan.  Bake approximately 45 minutes in 350-375ºF oven.

     Unlike true Soda Bread, this is a little moister and

great with butter or jelly and a cup of coffee.
